Steps

  1. Cut out templates and fabric
  2. Sew the side of the roll to the bottom
  3. Sew the cream spirals onto the cake fronts
  4. Stitch the cake fronts on, leaving a small opening
  5. Stuff the roll, then sew opening closed
  6. OPTIONAL Leaves
  7. OPTIONAL Strawberry
  8. OPTIONAL Cream
  9. OPTIONAL Blueberry
  10. OPTIONAL Attach decorations
Begin!

Step 1

Print and cut out pattern template. Cut fabric according to the template.

If you are making the optional decorations and are afraid you will lose some pieces or get them mixed up, consider pinning your paper template to your felt pieces!

You should have the following:

  • 1 cake bottom
  • 2 cake fronts
  • 1 cake side
  • 2 cream spiral
  • OPTIONAL 3 leaf pieces
  • OPTIONAL 1 strawberry piece
  • OPTIONAL 2 cream pieces
  • OPTIONAL 2 blueberry pieces
