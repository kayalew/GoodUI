Now you can have your own stuffed pastry! Sadly this one is not at all edible. This is a simple pattern with a lot of room for optional embellishments, if you're feeling adventurous!

Finished size: About 3 inches in diameter

Fabric

  • Pink felt, 6" x 4"
  • Brown felt, 12" x 2"
  • White felt, 5" x 2"

Other supplies

  • Pink and brown thread
  • Hand sewing needle
  • Scissors
  • Stuffing
  • Pattern templates
