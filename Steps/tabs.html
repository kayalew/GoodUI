Scrappy Starfish Beanbags

uploaded by Alice

For some silly fun in the sun, play catch, or ring toss with these colorful patchwork starfish beanbags-or even line up your beach pails for a friendly game of beanbag buckets. This sewing pattern is handy for last minute gifts to sew, as a beachy summer decoration or to keep handy for your sewing-for-kids needs.

Finished size: 9.5 inches in diameter

Adapted from Scrappy Starfish Beanbags by Amber Eden.

Fabric

  • 3 coordinating fat-quarter cotton print fabrics (makes 3 starfish)

Other supplies

  • 8 oz bag plastic pellets for filling (available at most craft supply stores)
  • Small funnel
  • Pattern templates
