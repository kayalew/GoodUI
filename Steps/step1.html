Strawberry Cream Roll

  • Overview
  • Instructions

Step 1

Print and cut out pattern template. Transfer pattern by tracing the template lightly on the appropriately colored felt and then cutting.

You should have the following:

  • 1 cake bottom
  • 2 cake fronts
  • 1 cake side
  • 2 cream spirals
